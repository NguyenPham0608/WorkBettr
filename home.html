Our Mission

Our mission is to help people get their work done on time, and thus build up good habits of time usage to combat procrastination

Our Product

We have developed an app to conquer the plague of procrastination. We do this by connecting different app users with each other to add a level of accountability. In addition to that, we are adding a standings system to increase motivation.

Growth of the WorkBettr Company

In 2019, WorkBettr was founded by four friends looking to solve the procrastination pandemic. They created the WorkBettr app to help people like themselves who struggled with laziness and procrastination that were preventing them from reaching their full potential. They felt held back. So they did something about it that could help everyone.
